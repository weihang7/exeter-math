Travel Information

Campus Map

Campus map and driving direction is available on Phillips Exeter Academy website here.

A printable campus map is available here.

Parking

Visitors to the Academy may park in the semi-circle in front of Jeremiah Smith Hall, in front of and behind Admissions (Bissell House), in any parking lot with assigned visitor parking and on nearby public streets. The closest parking for EMCC participants is the roadside parking on Front Street, Tan Lane, and other public streets nearby.

Transportation

For contestants, coaches, and parents flying in, please refer to Phillips Exeter Academy's "Transportation and Fare" web page for information here.

Lodging

We now provide 1 night of hotel stay at the local Fairfield Inn, Marriott for each traveling team traveling from outside a 300 mile radius from the school(3 rooms per team). We can also contact Flightline, an airport shuttle service to and from Boston and Manchester, if you would like; however, you will have to cover the expense of the shuttle yourself. If you are interested, please email us at emcc2014@gmail.com for details.
